Bastille is a British Indie Rock Band first established in 2010 by lead singer and songwriter Dan Smith. Other members of the band include Chris Wood, William Farquarson and Kyle Simmons. To date, they have released a single studio album ("Bad Blood") as well as a compilation, 2 EPs, and 2 mixtapes. Their singles "Pompeii" and "Bad Blood" have both charted, both in their native U.K. Singles Chart and the U.S. "Billboard Alternative Songs" chart. To date, they have sold over 2 million copies of their "Bad Blood" LP in the U.K. alone.
